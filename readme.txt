== Description ==

In this post i wanna tell you the alternative way for changing the look of your blog without worrying about the new positioning or other new widgets preparation. How? By changing the BackGround Image of course :-) Because the background image is the most visible part that always shown all over the page while we read the blog isn't it ? :-P

Let you change the look of your blog by changing the Background Image from your gallery

== Installation ==


1. Upload the zipped file to yoursite/wp-content/plugins
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Open up EasyIcon configuration tab in Option tab.
